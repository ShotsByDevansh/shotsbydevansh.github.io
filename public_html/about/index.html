About Us

The brand is about truth and creativity in a world that aims to shut it down. Don't be kept in someone else's box, create your own.

A brand is a statement and that is exactly what Bijoy Arora, Founder and Designer of Arora Apparel, set out to do. The company was founded independently by Bijoy in the winter of 2018. He has always been interested in fashion and, through his brand, has found the perfect medium to express it. His first release came in the form of a logo t-shirt and he has now expanded to hoodies and long-sleeves.

The brand is based in Pickering, Ontario, Canada and soon to expand.

He takes inspiration from many streetwear and vintage brands (Palace, Fear of God etc.) and even some local brands such as The Boy With Style. He also takes much inspiration from his Chrisitian background and looks to use this brand as a possible outreach.

The goal he has is to begin with a streetwear brand but hopefully advance into high-end fashion.
